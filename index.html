Project created for
PSAM 5550 Collab: One Plus
Optimum is creating ways to help you understand & manage your digital wellbeing.
Everyone has a different experience with technology and have different technological requirements. Optimum is an integrated android application that helps you balance your technological habits by providing a variety of tools you may need to achieve your personal usage goals.
Optimum builds on the existing google well-being features to enhance the user experience.
About optimum
Optimum is designed help you stay informed, motivated and focused.
Gen-Z users face trouble with focus as technology serves as a source distraction. Explore the new features to build a healthy relationship with technology and create new habits.
//001

Dashboard for more Insights

With complied data and graphs, understand how you use your phone in depth with daily, weekly, monthly and yearly.

View the number of phone unlocks, notifications and app timer over rides to identify patterns and optimize your usage.

//002

Accountability Settings

Help beat yours and your loved ones fear of missing out by sharing your usage insights and develop healthy habits together.

Allow them to hold you accountable for excessive screen time with nudges and vice versa.

//002

Track your productivity

Set timers to your task and track your completed tasks at the end of the day.

Curb your procrastination with push notifications & reminders.
